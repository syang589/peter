Research

I am broadly interested in algorithms, game theory, mechanism design, and machine learning theory. My current work studies data sharing and related incentive problems in multi-agent settings.
Pairwise Exchanges of Freely Replicable Goods with Negative Externalities
with Kirthevasan Kandasamy

We study how strategic agents exchange freely replicable goods when sharing creates negative externalities through competition. I designed a mechanism for pairwise exchange with provable incentive and efficiency guarantees.
